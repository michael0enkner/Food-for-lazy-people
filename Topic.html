Food for lazy people

In our poject "food for lazy people" we want to show you some recipes
With food for lazy people we don't mean that you take a chips pack sit on a
sofa and watch somthing on your TV or on your Computer rather we want you to
try some easy and fast recipes that taste better then some finished products
which you only put in your microwave or in your oven and make it warm.
We also want to inspire these people which think they can't cook to try it once
with one of our easy recipes or for these which want teach someone cooking
with these fast/easy recipes from our website.
